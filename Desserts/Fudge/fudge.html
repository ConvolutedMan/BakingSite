Fudge

While this recipe is pretty straightforward, it is very tempermental. I recommend reading all of the instructions before starting, because timing is super important here. Moreso than any of the other recipes on this site.

Quick facts

Difficulty: Difficult

Prep time: 20 minutes

Cok time: 15 minutes

Total time: 35 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 1/2 Cups sugar
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 1/2 Cup butter
  • 12 oz. semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 16 oz chocolate bars (I use Hershey’s © brand chocolate)
  • 2 Cups nuts (optional and an affront to good taste)
  • 7 oz marshmallow crème (if you don't have this, you can use 20 large marshmallows instead)
  • 1 Tsp. vanilla

instructions

  1. Before you get started, I recommend opening the marshmallow creme, the seal can be a pain sometimes. Also break the chocolate bars into pieces, and put those into a bowl with the chocolate chips. Once we start going, you won't have much time to stop.
  2. Mix and boil the sugar, evaporated milk, and butter. Stir constantly
  3. Once it starts boiling, let it go for 5 minutes. Stir constantly.
  4. At the 5 minute mark remove from heat and toss everything else in
  5. Stir until smooth
  6. Pour mixture into a buttered dish

helpful hints and tricks

Stir constantly! I can't stress this enough. If the sugar sits still too long it's likely to burn.

Also wear an oven mitt while you're stirring. I had a bubble pop while I was stirring and burned my finger

The 5 minutes of boiling is very imortant. If you go too long, you run the risk of burning the sugar, and if you don't go long enough it won't be hot enough to melt the chocolate
