Chocolate Snack Cake

This is a fairly easy recipe that makes a moist, dense cake. And it's technically vegan, so it's a great option if you're trying to be inclusive of your guests dietary preferences!

Quick facts

Difficulty: Easy

Prep time: 20 minutes

Bake time 40 minutes

Total time: 60 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 Cup flour
  • 1 Cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 Cup cocoa
  • 1 Tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 Tsp salt
  • 1 Cup water
  • 1/3 Cup oil
  • 1 Tsp. vinegar
  • 3/4 Tsp. vanilla
  • 1/2 Cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

instructions

  1. Grease an 8x8x2 cake pan.
  2. In a large bowl combine flour, brown sugar, cocoa, soda, and salt
  3. Add oil, water, vingar, and vanilla to dry ingredients, beat until smooth
