Aaron's cookie bars

This recipe comes courtesy of my friend Aaron who taught me this recipe when we were roommates in college

Quick facts

Difficulty: Intermediate

Prep time: 20 minutes

Bake time: 30 minutes

Total time: 50 minutes

Ingredients

Cookie

  • 1 Cup butter
  • 2 Cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 Tsp. vanilla
  • 2 1/2 Cups flour
  • 1 Tsp. baking soda
  • 1 Tsp. salt
  • 2 Cups oatmeal
  • 1 Cup nuts (optional)

Chocolate layer

  • 16 oz chocolate
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 Tbsp. butter
  • 1/2 Tsp. salt
  • 2 Tsp. vanilla

instructions

  1. Combine ingredients for cookie portion
  2. Mix together
  3. Pat down half of mixture into cake pan or cookie sheet
  4. Take ingredients for chocolate layer and melt together in a double boiler
  5. Pour chocolate mixture over cookie layer in pan
  6. Top with remaining cookie mixutre in random chunks
  7. Bake at 350Â° for 25-30 minutes
