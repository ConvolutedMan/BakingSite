Crepes (French Pancakes)

Quick facts

Difficulty: Intermediate

Prep time: 15 minutes

cook time 25 minutes

Total time: 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 Tbsp. sugar
  • 1/2 Tsp. baking powder
  • 1/2 Tsp. salt
  • 2 Cups milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 Tsp. vanilla
  • 2 Tbsp. butter or margarine (melted)

instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt into bowl
  2. Stir in remaining ingredients, beat until smooth
  3. Lightly butter 8 inch skillet, heat over medium heat until butter is bubbly
  4. Pour 1/4 Cup of batter into skillet. Immediately rotate pan until batter covers entire bottom of pan
  5. Cook until light brown, flip and brown on other side
