Lava baby cakes (They're so cute!)

Quick facts

Difficulty: Intermediate

Prep time: 60 minutes

Bake time 15 minutes

Total time: 75 minutes

Ingredients

Cake

  • 3/4 Cup butter
  • 6 oz. (1 Cup) semi-sweet chocolate pieces
  • 3 eggs
  • ingredient
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1/3 Cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 Tbsp. unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Powdered sugar (optional)

Filling

  • 3/4 Cup semi-sweet chocolate pieces
  • 2 Tbsp whipping cream

instructions

filling

  1. In heavy small saucepan combine chocolate pieces and whipping cream
  2. Cook and stir over low heat until chocolate melts
  3. Remove pan from heat
  4. Stir occassionally while cooling
  5. Chill for 30-45 minutes or until firm
  6. Form chilled filling into 6 equal balls
  7. Set aside, we'll use this after we prep the cake batter
  8. Lightly grease and flour six 3/4 Cup souffle dishes or 6oz. custard cups
  9. Place dishes in 15x10x1 pan and set aside

instructions

cake

  1. While making the filling, in a heavy medium saucepan, cook and stir butter and 1 cup of chocolate pieces over low heat until melted
  2. Remove from heat, cool
  3. In mixing bowl, beat eggs, egg yolks, granulated sugar, and vanilla with an electric mixer on high for 5 minutes, or until lemon colored
  4. Beat in chocolate mixture from step 1 of this section on medium speed
  5. Sift flour and cocoa over mixture; beat on low speed until just combined
  6. Fill prepared dishes with about 1/3 Cup of batter
  7. Place 1 ball of filling into each dish
  8. Spoon remaining batter into dishes
  9. Bake at 400Â° for about 13 minutes, or until cakes feel firm on edges
  10. Cool for 2-3 minutes
  11. Using a knife loosen cakes from side of dishes
  12. Invert onto plates
  13. Cover in powdered sugar
