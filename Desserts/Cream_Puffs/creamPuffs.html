Cream puffs (good luck)

Quick facts

Difficulty: Difficult

Prep time: 30 minutes

Bake time 25 minutes

Total time: 55 minutes

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 1 Cup water
  • 1/3 Tsp. salt
  • 1/2 Cup margarine
  • 1 Cup flour
  • 4 eggs

Sandwich filling

  • 8 oz. cream cheese
  • 1/3 Cup margarine
  • 1/2 - 3/4 Cup margarine
  • 2 Tbsp. chopped onion (fine)
  • 2 Tbsp. lemon juice
  • 1 Cup of meat (crab, chicken, shrimp, or ham)

instructions

Pastry

  1. In medium sauce pan combine margarine, water, and salt. Bring to a boil
  2. Add flour all at once, stirring vigourously
  3. Cook and stir until mixture forms a ball that doesn't separate
  4. Remove from heat
  5. Cool for 10 minutes
  6. Add eggs one at a time, beating with a wooden spoon after each addition until smooth
  7. Drop batter by small Tsp. size 3 inches apart on a greased baking sheet
  8. Bake at 400Â° for 25 minutes
  9. Remove from oven and place on cool on wire rack
  10. Split puffs and remove soft dough inside

Filling

  1. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl
  2. Fill split cream puffs
  3. Only good for 12 hours
  4. Dedcide you don't want this sandwich filling, because it's gross
  5. Fill cream puffs with whipped cream
