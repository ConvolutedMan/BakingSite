Cheesecake

Quick facts

Difficulty: Easy

Prep time: 10 minutes

Chill time 2 hours

Total time: 2 hours

Ingredients

  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 8 oz. cream cheese
  • 1/3 Cup lemon juice
  • 1 Tsp. vanilla
  • 1 store bought crust, because you sure as hell aren't making your own

instructions

  1. Blend sweetened condensed milk, vanilla, lemon juice, and cream cheese on low.
  2. Gradually increase speed until smooth
  3. Pour into crust
  4. CHill 2 hours in fridge, or 1 hour in the freezer, depending on how soon you want delicious cheesecake

helpful hints and tricks

ProTip: The filling doesn't set up as well if you use the low-fat versions of cream cheese
