Chocolate Brownies

Quick facts

Difficulty: Intermediate

Prep time: 25 minutes

Bake time 25 minutes

Total time: 50 minutes

Ingredients

Brownie

  • 2 Cups granulated sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 Tsp. vanilla
  • 2 Cups cake flour*
  • 2 oz. chocolate (prepare this separately)
    • 3 Tbsp. Cocoa
    • 1 Tbsp shortening
  • 1 1/2 Cup powdered sugar
  • 2/3 Cup oleo**
  • 1/2 Tsp. salt
  • 1 Tsp. baking powder

Frosting

  • 1 1/2 Cup powdered sugar
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 2 Tbsp hot water
  • 1 Tsp. vanilla
  • Dash of salt
  • 2-3 Tbsp. Cocoa

Instructions

Brownie

  1. Cream 2 cups of sugar, 4 eggs, and 2 Tsp. of vanilla for 2 minutes
  2. Add the cake flour, chocolate, salt, oleo, and baking powder
  3. Mis until batter is smooth
  4. Pour into prepared 13x9 pan
  5. Bake at 350Â° for 25 minutes

Frosting

  1. Beat all ingredients until smooth
  2. Frost cooled brownies

Footnotes

*Cake flour: remove Tbsp of flour per cup of flour and replace with 2 Tbsp cornstarch

**Oleo is technically margarine, but you can substitute for butter or vegetable shortening
