BBQ Chicken Wings

Quick facts

Difficulty: Intermediate

Prep time: 25 minutes

Cook time 1 hour 45 minutes

Total time: 2 hours

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs whole chicken wings
  • 2 Cups ketchup
  • 1/2 Cup honey
  • 2 Tbsp. lemon juice
  • 2 Tbsp. canola oil
  • 2 Tbsp. soy sauce
  • 2 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 Tbsp. paprika
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 1/2 Tsp. curry powder
  • 1/2 Tsp. pepper sauce

instructions

  1. Cut chicken wings into three sections, discard the inedible wing tips.
  2. Place wings in a greased 15 x 10 inch pan
  3. Bake at 350° for 35 – 40 minutes or until juices run clear
  4. In a large bowl, combine everything else
  5. Pour 1/2 Cup of sauce into slow cooker
  6. Drain wings, add to cooker
  7. Drizzle wings with remaining sauce
  8. Cook on low for an hour, baste that shit occasionally
