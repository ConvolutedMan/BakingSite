Cheese Ball

Quick facts

Difficulty: Intermediate

Prep time: 45 minutes

Cook time 0 minutes

Total time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 16 oz. softened cream cheese
  • 8 oz. shredded sharp cheddar
  • 1 Tbsp. finely chopped onions
  • 1 Tbsp. chopped red bell peppers
  • 2 Tsp. Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 Tsp. lemon juice
  • Dash of ground red pepper
  • Dash of salt
  • 1 Cup chopped pecans

Instructions

  1. Beat cream cheese with cheddar in small bowl like a red headed step child. Or use an electric mixer on medium until well blended
  2. Mix in all remaining ingredients except pecans, those come later
  3. Cover and refrigerate several hours or overnight
  4. Shape into balls, roll the balls in pecans and serve with crackers
