Pot roasted pork

Quick facts

Difficulty Intermediate:

Prep time: 30 minutes

Cook time 10 hours

Total time: 10 hours

Ingredients

  • 4 -5 lb. loin end pork roast
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, slivered
  • 2 medium onions, sliced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 whole clove
  • 1/2 Cup hot water
  • 1 Tbsp. soy sauce

instructions

  1. Rub pork roast with salt and pepper
  2. Make tiny slits in meat and insert slivers of garlic
  3. Place roast in broiler pan and broil 15 - 20 minutes to remove excess fat
  4. Put 1 sliced onion in bottom of crock pot
  5. Add browned pork roast and remaining onion and other ingredients
  6. Cover and cook on low 10 hours (5 - 8 hours on high)

helpful hints and tricks

To thicken gravy, remove roast and blend 2 Tbsp. cornstarch with 2 Tbsp. cold water to form a smooth paste. Set the crock pot on high and pour in the paste. Stir well and let come to a boil, until thickened.
