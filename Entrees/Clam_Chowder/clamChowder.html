Manhattan clam chowder (Chowda!)

Quick facts

Difficulty: Easy

Prep time: 10 minutes

Cook time: 10 hours

Total time: 10 hours

Ingredients

  • 1/4 lb bacon, diced and fried
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, thinly sliced
  • 3 stalks celery, sliced
  • 1 Tbsp. dired parsley flakes
  • 1 28 oz can tomatoes
  • 1 Tsp. salt
  • 2 to 3 clans clams (7 oz each) with liquid
  • 2 whole peppercorns
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 Tsp. dried thyme leaves, crushed
  • 2 to 3 medium potatoes, pared and diced

instructions

  1. Put all ingredients in crock pot
  2. Cover and cook on low 8-10 hours (3-5 hours on high)
