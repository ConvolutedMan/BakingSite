Roasted Chicken

Quick facts

Difficulty: Easy

Prep time: 20 minutes

Cook time 60 minutes

Total time: 80 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 stalk celery (it is imperative you have this)
  • 1 (3 1/2 lb.) roasting chicken (this is optional)
  • 1/2 Tsp. dried thyme
  • 1/2 Tsp. salt
  • 1 Tbsp. butter, softened
  • 1 onion, quartered

instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 475°
  2. Slice celery
  3. Sprinkle inside cavity of chicken with thyme and salt
  4. Add 1 Tsp. butter, sliced celery, and onion to cavity of chicken
  5. Rub outside of chicken with remaining butter
  6. Place chicken on pan breast side down
  7. Add enough water to cover bottom of pan
  8. Roast for 10 minutes
  9. Reduce temperature to 375°
  10. Roast for additional 20 minutes
  11. Turn chicken breast side up
  12. Roast until browned, about 30 minutes

Alternative preparation

Alternatively you can buy one fully cooked from your local grocer
