Seafood Chowder

Quick facts

Difficulty: Easy

Prep time: 20 minutes

Cook time: 9 hours

Total time: 9 hours

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs fresh or frozen fish filets, but not both
  • 3 slices bacon, diced
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1/2 to 3/4 Cup chopped green onion, with tops
  • 2-3 potatoes, pared and cubed
  • 1 1/2 Cups water
  • 1 1/2 Tsp. salt
  • 1/4 Tsp. pepper
  • 1 13 oz can evaporated milk

instructions

  1. Cut fish filets into bite size pieces (if using frozen, thaw first)
  2. In small skillet, saut√© bacon and onion until golden
  3. Drain skillet and put in crock pot with fish
  4. Add remaining ingredients except evaporated milk
  5. Cover and cook on low for 6-9 hours (2.5-3.5 hours on high)
  6. Add evaporated milk during last hour
