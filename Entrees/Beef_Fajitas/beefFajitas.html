Beef fajitas

Quick facts

Difficulty: Easy

Prep time: 10 minutes

Cook time 4-5 hours

Total time: 4-5 hours

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs beef round steak
  • 14 oz can of diced tomatoes, drained
  • 1 onion sliced
  • 1 green pepper, sliced into strips
  • 1 red pepper, sliced into strips
  • 1 jalpeno, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 Tsp. fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 Tsp. chili powder
  • 1 Tsp. ground cumin
  • 1 Tsp. ground coriander
  • 1/4 Tsp. salt
  • 8-10 6" flour tortillas

instructions

  1. Place beef in a slow cooker
  2. In a separate bowl combine vegetables and seasonings; spoon over beef
  3. Cook on low for 8-10 hours, or on high for 4-5 hours
  4. Shred beef
  5. Serve with tortillas
