Biscuits and Country Gravy

Quick facts

Difficulty: Easy

Prep time: 5 minutes

Cook time 20 minutes

Total time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • Buy biscuits (serously it's way easier)
  • 8 oz. age or maple flavored bulk sausage
  • 1/4 Cup (1/2 stick) butter
  • 1/3 Cup all purpose flour
  • 2 Cups milk
  • 1 Cup water
  • 1 Tsp. salt
  • 1 Tsp. ground black pepper

instructions

  1. Cook and stir sausage in skillet over medium heat, crumbling it with spoon. Drain fat if necessary
  2. Melt butter in medium sauce pan over medium heat
  3. Stir in flour, stirring constantly to prevent scorching
  4. Cook until the roux begins to darken slightly. Roux means the butter flour mixture, if you didnâ€™t know. I totally knew that.
  5. Slowly whisk in milk, water, salt, and pepper
  6. Continue stirring until gravy thickens
  7. stir in sausage
  8. sPour over the biscuits
