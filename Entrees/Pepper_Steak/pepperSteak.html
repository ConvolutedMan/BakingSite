Pepper sirloin steak

Quick facts

Difficulty: Intermediate

Prep time: 15 minutes

Cook time 15 minutes

Total time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 lb. sirloin steak, 1 1/2 inches thick
  • 1/2 Cup butter
  • 1/4 Cup chopped fresh parsley or 4 Tsp. dried parsley
  • 1/4 Cup minced onion
  • 2 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 Tsp. freshly ground pepper
  • 1/2 Tsp. dry mustard

instructions

  1. Prehead grill or broiler, otherwise the grill or broiler will be cold
  2. Lightly score edges of steak at 1 inch intervals
  3. Combine butter, parsley, onion, Worcestershire sauce, pepper, and mustard in a small saucepan
  4. Heat mixture over low heat until butter melts, stirring continuously
  5. Reserve 1/4 of the mixture
  6. Place steak on grill or broiler, brush with butter mixture.
  7. Cook about 6 minutes per side, basting freqently (this is for medium, cooking time varies)
  8. Place steak on serving platter and cut thin slices across the grain
  9. Drizzle reserved butter mixture over steak
