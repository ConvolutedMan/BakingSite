Shepherd's Pie

Quick facts

Difficulty: Easy

Prep time: 25 minutes

Bake time 45 minutes

Total time: 70 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. ground beef
  • 1/2 onion chopped
  • Garlic powder and sesoning salt to taste
  • 3/4 oz/ package brown gravy mix
  • 2 10 3/4 oz. cans of cream of mushroom soup
  • 2 1/2 Cups of water
  • 1 1/2 Cup frozen sliced carrots, thawed
  • 10 oz/ package frozen peas, thawed
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 1/2 - 3 Cups poatoes, peeled, cooked, and mashed
  • ingredient

instructions

  1. Brown beef and onion in a skillet, seson to taste with garlic powder and seasoning salt
  2. Drain and pour into large bowl
  3. Stir in gravy mix, soup, water, carrots, and peas. Mix well
  4. Spoon into greased 9 x 13 pan
  5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
  6. Spread mashed potatoes over top
  7. Bake at 350Â° for 45 minutes
  8. Sprinkle with paprika
