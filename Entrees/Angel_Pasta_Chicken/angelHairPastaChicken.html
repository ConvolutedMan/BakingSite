Angel hair pasta and chicken

Quick facts

Difficulty: Intermediate

Prep time: 15 minutes

Cook time: 40 minutes

Total time: 55 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 skinless boneless chicken breast halves, cut into 1 inch cubes
  • 2 Tbsp. olive oil, divided
  • 1 carrot sliced diagonally into 1/4 inch pieces
  • 10 oz frozen brocolli florets, thawed
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 12 oz angel hair pasta (disappointingly this is not made of actual angel hair)
  • 2/3 Cup chicken broth
  • 1 Tsp. dried basil
  • 1/4 Cup grated parmesean cheese

instructions

  1. Heat 1 Tbsp. of olive oil in a medium skillet over medium heat
  2. Add chicken, cook until chicken is cooked thoroughly
  3. Remove from skillet and drain on paper towels
  4. Heat remaining oil in same skillet
  5. Begin heating water for pasta
  6. Add carrot to skillet
  7. Cook for 4 minutes, stirring occasionally
  8. Add garlic and brocolli to skillet, cook for 2 minutes, stirring occasionally
  9. Cook pasta according to package
  10. While pasta is cooking, add broth, basil, and parmesean to skillet. Stir
  11. Return chicken to skillet
  12. Reduce heat and simmer for 4 minutes
  13. Drain pasta, place in bowl
  14. Top pasta with chicken and vegetable mixture
  15. Serve immediately
  16. Or don't. Whatever. I'm not the food police
