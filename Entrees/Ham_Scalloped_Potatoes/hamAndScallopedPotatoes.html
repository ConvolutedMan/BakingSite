Ham and scalloped potatoes

Quick facts

Difficulty: Intermediate

Prep time: 20 minutes

Cook time: 10 hours

Total time: 10 hours

Ingredients

  • 6 - 8 slices of ham
  • 8 -10 medium potatoes, pared and thinly sliced
  • 2 onions, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 Cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1 10 oz. can cream of celery or mushroom soup
  • 1/2 Tsp. cream of tartar
  • 1 Cup water
  • Paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

instructions

  1. Toss sliced potatoes in cream of tartar and water
  2. Drain
  3. Put half of ham, potatoes, and onions in crock pot
  4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
  5. Sprinkle with grated cheese
  6. Repeat with remaining half
  7. Spoon undiluted soup over top
  8. Sprinkle with paprika
  9. Cover and cook in crock pot on low 8-10 hours (4 hours on high)
