Cuban pork chops

Quick facts

Difficulty: Intermediate

Prep time: 15 minutes

Cook time 15 minutes

Total time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 8oz. pork chops
  • 4 large garlic cloves
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 Cup fresh lime juice
  • 1/4 Tsp. ground cumin
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 Cup water

instructions

  1. Thinly slice garlic
  2. Dry pork chops with paper towels
  3. Season generously with salt
  4. Heat olive oil in large skillet over medium heat
  5. Stand and brown fat edges of pork chops, about 2 minutes
  6. Brown first side, about 2 minutes
  7. Turnover and brown, 1 minute more
  8. Scatter garlic around pork chops and cook, stirring for about 15 seconds or so
  9. Add lime juice and water
  10. Cook uncovered, turning chops once or twice until done and 1/4 syrupy juices remain. About 6 - 8 minutes
  11. Transfer chops to plates
  12. Spoon sauce on top
  13. Sprinkle with cumin and season with pepper
  14. Serve hot

helpful hints and tricks

If the pan juices reduce too much before the pork chops are done, add more water. If you add too much water, just boil down the juices after the chops are out of the pan
