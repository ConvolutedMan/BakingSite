Chili

Quick facts

Difficulty: Easy

Prep time: 20 minutes

Cook time 2 hours

Total time: 2 hours

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. extra-lean ground beef, cooked and drained
  • 1 Tbsp. oil
  • 1 29 oz. can tomato sauce
  • 1 29 oz. can of pinto beans, use liquid
  • 1 Cup dried onion
  • 2 small cans chopped green chiles
  • 1 stalk celery, finely diced
  • 2 Tsp. cumin
  • 2 Tsp. chili powder
  • 2 Tsp. salt
  • 2 Cups water

instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients and simmer for two hours in frying pan
