Enchiladas

Quick facts

Difficulty: Intermediate

Prep time: 25 minutes

Cook time 30 minutes

Total time: 55 minutes

Ingredients

  • 12 homemade style large flour tortillas
  • 1 large can enchilada sauce
  • 1 lb ground beef, cooked and drained (optional)
  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 Tbsp. canned diced green chiles
  • 1 lb grated cheddar cheese
  • 16 oz cottage cheese
  • 2 Cups sour cream

instructions

  1. Dip each tortilla in hot oil for about 30-60 seconds to soften
  2. Place between paper towels to absorb grease
  3. Add onion to ground beef, cook and drain
  4. Combine cottage cheese, sour cream, and green chiles in a bowl
  5. In a 9x13 pan place a tortilla, add 1 Tbsp. beef, 2 Tbsp. cottage cheese mix, 1 Tbsp. cheddar cheese, and a spoonful of enchilada sauce
  6. Roll the tortilla and place at one end of the pan
  7. Continue until pan is full. For a more hardcore option, keep going after the pan is full and really stack those mofo's!
  8. Top with enchilada sauce and cheese
  9. Bake at 350Â° for 30 minutes or until bubbly
  10. Serve with sour cream
