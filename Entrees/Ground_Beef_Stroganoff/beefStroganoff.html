Ground beef stroganoff

Quick facts

Difficulty: Intermediate

Prep time: 20 minutes

Cook time: 9 hours

Total time: 9 hours

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 2 medium onions, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 4oz. can sliced mushrooms, drained
  • 1 1/2 Tsp. salt
  • 1/4 Tsp. pepper
  • 1 Cup beef consomm√© or bouillon
  • 3 Tbsp. tomato paste
  • 3/4 Cup sour cream mixed with 2 Tbsp. flour
  • ingredient

instructions

  1. Brown ground beef in large skillet
  2. Add onions, garlic, and mushrooms to skillet
  3. Sautee until onion is golden brown
  4. Put in crock pot with all remaining ingredients except sour cream
  5. Stir thoroughly
  6. Cover and cook on low 7-9 hours (3 hours on high)
  7. Stir in sour cream 1 hour before serving
  8. Serve over hot buttered noodles or rice
