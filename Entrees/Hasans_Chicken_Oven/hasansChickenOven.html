Hasan's chicken in the oven

This recipe comes courtesy of my friend Hasan. He taught me this recipe when we lived together in college. This is one of my favorite ways to have chicken prepared.However be warned that this is the least precise recipe on the site, so you'll have to play it by ear.

Quick facts

Difficulty: Intermediate

Prep time: 20 minutes

Bake time minutes

Total time: minutes

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts or chicken legs
  • Potatoes
  • Mushrooms (optional)
  • 2-3 lemons
  • Salt and pepper
  • Garlic (optional)

instructions

  1. Cut potatoes into round slices, place on bottom of deep cake pan
  2. Place chicken over potatoes
  3. Top with salt, pepper, and a lot of olive oil.
  4. Add more olive oil, until you feel the chicken is half sinking (this is a Hasan approved step)
  5. Cut garlic and place on chicken to taste
  6. Top with mushrooms
  7. Drizzle lemon juice over entire pan to taste
  8. Put in oven at 375Â° for 2 hours, or until chicken is cooked
