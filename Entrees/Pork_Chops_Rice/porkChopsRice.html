Pork Chops and rice

Quick facts

Difficulty: Easy

Prep time: 15 minutes

Cook time 2 hours

Total time: 2 hours 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 pork chops
  • 1 pkg dry onion soup mix
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 2 1/2 cans water (use soup can)
  • 1 cup rice (uncooked)

instructions

  1. Brown pork chops
  2. Put rice in a pan, I use a 9x13
  3. Arrange pork chops over rice
  4. Sprinkle on onion soup mix
  5. Spoon on mushroom soup
  6. Add water
  7. Cover pan with foil
  8. Bake at 350Â° for 1 1/2 - 2 hours
