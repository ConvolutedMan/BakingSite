Spanish rice

Quick facts

Difficulty: Easy

Prep time: 20 minutes

Cook time: 9 hours

Total time: 9 hours

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ground chuck or beef
  • 2 medium onions, chopped
  • ingredient
  • 2 green peppers, chopped
  • 1 28 oz can tomatoes
  • 1 8 oz can tomato sauce
  • 1 Cup water
  • 2 1/2 Tsp. chili powder
  • 2 Tsp. salt
  • 2 Tsp. Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 cup raw rice (coverted)(This rice has seen the light. Praise Jesus!)

instructions

  1. Brown beef in skillet and drain off fat
  2. Put all ingredients in crock pot, stir thoroughly
  3. Cover and cokk on low for 7-9 hours (3 hours on high)
